MINIMUM ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS
In order to be admitted to a program leading to a Diploma of College Studies (DEC), applicants must possess a Quebec Secondary School Diploma or have completed a level of education that is deemed equivalent by the College. Applicants must also have successfully completed Mathematics TS 5 or SN 5. Students who have Math CST 4 will also be considered and, if admitted, will take the equivalent to Math TS 5 or SN 5 as an extra course.
For detailed admissions requirements please refer to the Admissions Handbook.

ADMISSION PRIORITIES
Even if candidates to programs meet the requirements of the Règlement sur le regime des études collégiales, they may be refused admission because of the lack of space.
